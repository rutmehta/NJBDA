  Electronic Business

  Credits:     3

  Prerequisites:    3

  New Course   Spring



  Dr. Robert Hancox

  hancoxcccnj.edu

  CS 219 Electronic Business: Strategies and Technologies (3:3:0)  Students

will explore how the Internet and

   on-line technology is impacting business enterprises.  Students will

focus on the relationship between

  corporate strategies and technology, specifically how technology can

directly contribute to marketing

  results.  In addition, students will gain an appreciation for the value

in successfully launching and

  managing an e-business Web site.
