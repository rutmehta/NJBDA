  CS 181 Project Management (3:3:0)  This course provides students with

information about working

  successfully in a project environment, including how to organize and

manage effective project

  developments within and outside the team.  In-depth coverage of planning,

scheduling, and cost estimating

   is also included.  Students will use Project Management software and

Internet-related exercises.
