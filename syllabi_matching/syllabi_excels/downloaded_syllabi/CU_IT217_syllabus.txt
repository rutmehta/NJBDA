modeling using Autodesk Mechanical

  Desktop software.













  COURSE OBJECTIVES

  Upon successful completion of this course the student will be able to:

create parametric solids,

  assemblies, surfaces, and table driven parts and bills of materials.  The

student will be also able to prepare

   designs for final documentation.
